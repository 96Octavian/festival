Reservations

Events

You're expected to reserve or book a seat for every event you're willing to attend. To avoid crowded halls or long queues, reservation for events is only possible via the online procedure on the website.
Events are all held in our main hall, easily rechable following onsite directions. Your online reservations grants you access to any exhibition that composes an event

Seminars

Seminars, unlike events, do not require prior booking.
In the seminar description you'll find the location where teh seminar will be held, and once there you'll find a registration spot where you will be allowed to register.
